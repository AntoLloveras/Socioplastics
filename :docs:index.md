## Overview

Socioplastics is a transdisciplinary research framework initiated in 2009 and developed as a structured epistemic system across architecture, urban theory, systems thinking, media theory, and critical spatial practice.

The repository is organised as a public research infrastructure composed of indexed nodes, structured books, three tomes, and DOI-anchored conceptual cores.

Current status:

- 3,000 indexed nodes
- 30 books
- 3 tomes
- 6 conceptual cores
- DOI-anchored research objects
- Public semantic field

---

## Structure

- `/books` — CenturyPack books (100 nodes each)
- `/nodes` — indexed node structure
- `/ontology` — conceptual definitions and field grammar
- `/structure` — scalar architecture and system organisation
- `/data` — datasets, manifests, and exports
- `/infrastructure` — schemas, protocols, and semantic support
