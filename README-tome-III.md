Foundation, development, and execution converge into one scalar architecture. The tome closes at Node 3000 — Executive Mode — achieving operational closure without exhaustion.

## Core Layers — Tome III

Tome III contains three DOI-anchored ontological cores — the highest concentration in the corpus. Core IV activates the field as a self-authorising formation (Nodes 2501–2510). Core V seals the legibility infrastructure (Nodes 2901–2910). Core VI closes the 3000-node cycle with the ten terminal nodes (Nodes 2991–3000). All three in Zenodo. Together they form 3% of the tome's nodes. The asymmetry across the three tomes — two cores, one core, three cores — is structural, not accidental: it reflects the increasing DOI density as the corpus moves from foundation toward execution.
